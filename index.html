I'm Idowu Michael, a self-driven Full Stack Developer based in Nigeria, building for the web with a focus on React, Node.js, and MongoDB. What started as curiosity about how websites work turned into a genuine passion for crafting digital experiences that actually matter.

My journey has been hands-on from day one. I didn't wait for the perfect course or the perfect moment — I started building. From my first gadget store to a food delivery app with real geolocation maps and Paystack payment integration — every project taught me something a tutorial never could.
